1. Renter Management

Req IDRequirementPriorityStatus
RM-001 Primary Leaseholder
Capture renter's full name, unit/lot number, monthly rent amount, phone number, and email address. 		High Complete
RM-002 Co-Leaseholder
Allow a co-leaser to be added for each renter with name, email, and phone number fields. 		High Complete
RM-003 Active/Inactive Status
Ability to mark renters as inactive (e.g., moved out, evicted) without deleting their records. Inactive renters should not appear in active lists or generate invoices. 		High Complete
RM-004 Document Attachments — Lease
Attach external documents (PDF, DOC, images) to each renter's profile. Primary use case: uploading the signed lease agreement for each unit. Files stored in AWS S3 (max 5 MB). 		High Complete

2. Invoices

Req IDRequirementPriorityStatus
INV-001 Auto-Generate Invoices
Automatically create invoices for all active renters on the 1st of each month. Invoices include monthly rent as the primary line item. Duplicate invoices for the same period should not be created. 		High Complete
INV-002 Editable Invoices
Allow editing of invoice details including date, due date, period, notes, and individual line items (description, quantity, unit price). Line items can be added or removed. 		High Complete
INV-003 Late Fee — Day 6 (10% + $75 Magistrate)
On the 6th of each month (1 day past the 5th due date), automatically apply:
  • 10% late fee calculated on the base rent amount
  • $75.00 Magistrate/Court fee
Auto-applied via AWS EventBridge + Lambda on the 6th of each month. 		High Complete
INV-004 Late Fee — Day 10 (Additional 10%)
On the 10th of each month (5 days past due), automatically apply an additional 10% fee calculated on the total of rent + Day 6 late fee (excluding the $75 Magistrate fee). Auto-applied via AWS EventBridge + Lambda on the 10th of each month. 		High Complete
INV-005 Document Attachments — Court Docs
Attach external documents (PDF, DOC, images) to individual invoices. Primary use case: attaching court documents, legal notices, or correspondence related to a specific invoice. Files stored in AWS S3 (max 5 MB). 		High Complete

3. Credits & Overpayments

Req IDRequirementPriorityStatus
CR-001 Overpayment Detection
If a renter pays more than the total due (monthly rent + fees + additional charges), the overpayment amount is automatically recorded as a credit in the renter's credit balance. 		High Complete
CR-002 Apply Credits to Future Invoices
Allow applying a renter's credit balance toward any unpaid invoice. The invoice view shows the available credit with an "Apply Credit" button. Credits are deducted from the balance and applied as payment. 		High Complete
CR-003 Credits & Refunds Management
Dedicated page to view, filter, and manage all credits and refunds by renter, type (credit/refund/adjustment), and date range. Manual credit entries supported. 		High Complete

4. Receipts

Req IDRequirementPriorityStatus
RC-001 Editable Receipts
Create and edit receipts with multiple line items. Changes to receipts automatically recalculate the renter's payment balance for the affected month. Credits are kept separate from charge items when editing. 		High Complete
RC-002 Receipt Balance Tracking
Receipts list shows total charges, total credits, and balance (remaining due or overpayment credit) per receipt. Renter's overall credit balance is displayed. 		High Complete
RC-003 Credit Recording on Receipts
When creating or editing a receipt, allow credits to be recorded (e.g., overpayment, returned items). Credits are stored in the credits table and displayed separately on the receipt. 		High Complete

5. Reports

Req IDRequirementPriorityStatus
RPT-001 Account Statements
Generate account statements per renter showing invoices, receipts, credits, total charges, total payments, and balance. Filterable by month and year. Printable format. 		High Complete
RPT-002 Monthly Closing Statement
Generate end-of-month closing statements showing total expected rent, collected amounts, outstanding balances, and payment status per renter. 		High Complete
RPT-003 Petty Cash / Coins Report
Monthly petty cash report grouped by category showing inflows, outflows, and net balance. Exportable to PDF via print-friendly view. 		Medium Complete

6. User Management & Security

Req IDRequirementPriorityStatus
USR-001 Users & Passwords
Admin can create and manage user accounts with roles (admin, manager, viewer). Users have username, display name, and password. Passwords are securely hashed. 		High Complete
USR-002 Forgot Password / Security Question
Users can recover their password using a security question set during account setup. Accessible from the login page. 		Medium Complete
USR-003 Login Required
All pages require authentication. Unauthorized access redirects to login page. 		High Complete

7. Additional Features

Req IDRequirementPriorityStatus
ADD-001 Fee Schedule
Configurable fee schedule (under Settings) with predefined fee types and amounts. Fees can be added as line items on receipts and invoices. 		Medium Complete
ADD-002 Dashboard
Monthly overview showing expected rent, collected amounts, outstanding balances, and payment rates across all renters. 		Medium Complete
ADD-003 Deposits Tracking
Track bank deposit confirmations for receipts with deposit date and confirmation status. 		Low Complete
